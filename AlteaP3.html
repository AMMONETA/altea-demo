Why This Pipeline is Critical for Altea‚Äôs Future

1. The Strategic Impact of Data Centralization

Instead of pulling partial numbers from multiple Odoo modules, every KPI will come from one governed, unified dataset. This eliminates "spreadsheet chaos" and ensures everyone is working from the same figures, with the same definitions. With data refreshed as often as every 15 minutes, decisions are always based on up-to-date information.

2. Bypassing Odoo Online's Limitations

Odoo Online does not allow direct database access for heavy reporting. Any attempt to do so would be slow and affect live operations. This pipeline offloads all reporting queries to BigQuery, keeping Odoo fast and responsive. It effectively unlocks your data from Odoo's closed system into a warehouse you control.

3. A Foundation for Future Advantage

This pipeline is not just a reporting tool; it's a long-term asset. Once built, it can integrate other systems (CRMs, supplier portals), support predictive models like demand forecasting, and even power client-facing dashboards, creating a unique differentiator in Altea‚Äôs market.
